This folder contains copies of documents originally published on NikonPassion following a discussion started in this thread

Unfortunately, the articles are not online anymore and images go missing in the versions available on the wayback machine: part 1 and part 2

NikonPassion admins have been contacted to request copies of the original articles, but they have not replied to date.

User ubmtdc kindly offered to send us pdf captures of those pages, as well as translations thereof. In detail:

  • Interprétation des signaux boîtier-objectif 1.pdf : part 1 of the original article in French by Gazalou
  • Interprétation des signaux boîtier-objectif 2.pdf : part 2 of the original article in French by Gazalou
  • Body-Lens signal interpretation 1 - Translated.pdf : English translation of part 1 of the original article
  • Body-Lens signal interpretation 2 - Translated.pdf : English translation of part 2 of the original article
  • orig.translated.c : source code of an early C code prototype by Gazalou to emulate a lens with a PIC, with most of the original French comments translated into English
  • pins.txt : pinout information collected by Joseph Wisniewski

This folder is referred to by this post on Nikonhacker.com forum.

If you are an original author of one of these articles (first thank you very much :-)), please don't hesitate to contact us in case you want some information added, corrected or removed.
